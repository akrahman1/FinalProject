About Our Café

Café;Modern, started in Indianapolis, Indiana in 1994, and quickly evolved into a full service cafe. We have some famous Salads, fresh roasted coffee and made-from-scratch favorites. People come from far and wide to try our fresh house-made Soups, Salads, Sandwiches and Desserts! Whether you are stopping by for specialty coffee or joining us for breakfast or lunch – Café;Modern always offers the freshest food and coffee around.
